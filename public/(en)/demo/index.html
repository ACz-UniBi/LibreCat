Embed Publication Lists

Publication lists which are maintained in PUB, can easily be embedded in other contexts such as personal homepages. Publications lists of working groups, chairs or faculties can be embedded as well.

After the login in PUB you'll find the menu option "Embed" in the navigation bar. The procedure is explained step by step. A generator for creating source code for static links, javascript code and iFrame Code and a choice of citation styles is provided.

Portals for University Departments

You can easily implement the embed options which are described above on your own. In addition to that, we offer to build a "departmental portal" for the presentation of publication activities of complete departments or working groups on request.

The presentation of the publication list will be enhanced by several filter options, e.g. authors, subdivisions, year, publication type, citation style, language.
A full text search can be implemented and the corporate design of the respective department will be adapted to the list page.
The portal will be integrated into the department's web pages and because of the link to PUB, it is always up to date.

Examples of portals which are already in operation:

  • Bielefeld Graduate School in History and Sociology (BGHS)
  • Center of Excellence Cognitive Interaction Technology (CITEC)
  • Faculty of Educational Science
  • Faculty of Biology
  • Faculty of Biology, Department of Cognitive Neuroscience
  • Faculty of Biology, Department of Neurobiology

Further portals are under way.

Please contact us if you want a portal for your department!
